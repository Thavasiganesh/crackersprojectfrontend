SAFETY TIPS

Before Use

  • Read all instructions carefully
  • Keep water bucket nearby
  • Use in open areas only
  • Check wind direction

During Use

  • Light at arm's length
  • Never hold in hand
  • Move away immediately
  • Never relight duds

After Use

  • Soak used fireworks
  • Dispose properly
  • Check for embers
  • Keep area clean
